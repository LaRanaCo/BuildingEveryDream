Custom Home Building

We build custom homes from the ground up across Texas — working closely with you from design and permitting all the way to move-in day. No cookie-cutter builds. Every home is designed around how you actually live.

  • Full custom home design & build
  • Site preparation, grading & foundations

  • Electrical, plumbing & HVAC coordination
  • Permit acquisition & inspection management

Drainage & Gutters

Poor drainage is one of the most damaging and overlooked issues for Texas properties. We design systems that move water away from your foundation before it causes expensive problems.

  • Seamless aluminum & steel gutter installation
  • Gutter guards & leaf protection
  • French drains & channel drains
  • Yard grading & slope correction
  • Downspout extensions & underground routing
  • Drainage problem diagnosis
Texas Drainage TipClay-heavy North Texas soil doesn't absorb water well. Pooling within 6 feet of your foundation after rain is the leading cause of foundation shifts.
🌧️

Foundation Protection Starts at the Roof

Proper drainage is the best foundation insurance money can buy.

Wood & Metal Fencing

We build fences that define your property, provide real privacy, and hold up against Texas weather — from cedar privacy fences to ornamental iron and automated gates.

  • Cedar & treated pine privacy fencing
  • Ornamental iron & steel fencing
  • Chain-link (residential & commercial)
  • Automated driveway gates
  • Fence repair, board replacement & staining
  • Survey-based property line installation

Wood vs. Metal: What's Right for You?

Wood offers warmth and privacy at lower cost. Metal provides decades of low-maintenance durability.
